About Angela Row Houses

5 classic new Craftsman style homes in downtown Pleasanton.

These are the perfect homes for executives. To move in, all you need are your clothes and your laptop.

Completely furnished, down to the linens and tableware, everything is ready for your residency. Leases start from 3 months and can last up to a year! These fine Craftsmen style homes are built with the latest in technology and nearly everything is provided for you upon move in. Below is a rundown of the amenities, but this list by no means covers it all. Schedule an appointment to view all they have to offer.

  • 1 Block from downtown Pleasanton with unique shops and over 25 restaurants
  • Less than one block from the local Farmer’s Market each Saturday
  • 1 Block from the Pleasanton Library and city offices
  • 2 Blocks from the Pleasanton Police Department
  • 2 and 3 bedroom homes with 2 to 3 bathrooms each
  • 600 sq. ft., 2 car garages with epoxy urethane coated floors
  • Garage level, under-stairs storage room, perfect for sports equipment
  • Low electric bills, these homes are solar powered
  • ERV energy recover ventilators circulate each home’s entire air system in 24 hours
  • Endless supply of hot water with the tankless water heater
  • Dual zone heating/cooling with as much as 8°­ difference between upstairs & downstairs
  • Ceiling fans in all bedrooms
  • Granite throughout, including window sills in kitchens and baths
  • Hardwood floors throughout, carpeted stairs
  • Each kitchen has a pantry, 3 have walk-in pantries
  • Double pane windows with shutters, shades, and room coordinating draperies
  • Living rooms each have 65” 3D Compatible Samsung HDTVs
  • Electronics include WIFI, cable TV, & BluRay players
  • Each bedroom also has a wall mounted 50” TV
  • All appliances are top of the line GE Profile and energy efficient
  • Each home is alarmed for security purposes
  • Angela Row Houses are 2 blocks from the ACE Train station, with travel to San Jose
  • County Connection buses stop on Peters Avenue
